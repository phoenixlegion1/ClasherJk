Hello everyone, this message is difficult to share. After much thought, I’ve decided to temporarily close BOSS OF COC.

We’ve constantly had to remind members to complete clan games, capital attacks, war attacks, and donations, but things still haven’t been running as they should. With over 50% players not completing capital attacks, inactive, not completing clan games and war donations it’s become overwhelming and frustrating for me. managing things has become challenging and exhausting.

This isn’t a decision I make lightly. Many of you have been loyal, committed, and exceptional players, and I truly appreciate your dedication. It’s because of members like you that I’m confident we’ll come back stronger.

Right now, I need to take a step back and recharge, so this pause is essential for both my well-being and our clan’s future.

When we return, we’ll be refreshed, refocused, and ready to rebuild with a stronger, more dedicated team. Thank you for your loyalty and understanding.

CWL will not be held in BOSS OF COC untill it's rebuild. If you want to play CWL, join #29Y02C80P. CWL will start on November 1st there.
